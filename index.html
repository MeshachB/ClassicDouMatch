Classic Duos Matchup Game

How to Play

Welcome to the Classic Duos Match Game!
You have 3 minutes to match all 25 iconic duos from pop culture.
Click on any two cards to reveal their names. If they’re a matching duo, they’ll stay face up!
If not, they’ll flip back over after a moment.

Try to match all the duos before time runs out. Good luck, and have fun!
